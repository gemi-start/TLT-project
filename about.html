“Amidst the nature & the beauty of the world, a magic seed was planted.”

In a city that boasts with heart and love for good times, a magic seed was planted. Conceived with the first restaurant on the banks of the River Nile, and growing to become the Multidisciplinary TLT Concepts. The Lemon Tree Family of brands include a diversified portfolio offering nothing short from excellence in hospitality, retail, entertainment, concept design, event management, signature branded residences
& more...

All for which work in harmony to produce tasteful experiences to those who seek refinement & quality.

branches of a tree

TLT Concepts is profoundly growing while honouring the highest standards of simple & functional luxury living. It continuously thrives on passion & ambition & although TLT Concepts started off as a leading entity in food & beverage, offering a seasonally curated menus and enticing events; the brand has evolved further by developing into 4 distinctive branches from the tree:

  • HOSPITALIT >
  • TLT LIVING >
  • TLT EVENTS & ENTERTAINMENT >
  • CAN LIMÓN >
