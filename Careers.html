"To making your own way & exploring new opportunities."

The Lemon Tree & Co. Group is profoundly growing, with a range of 15+ leading brands in the hospitality and entertainment markets, both locally and internationally. It is a world of opportunities and an endless line of inspirations just waiting to unfold.

A magic seed was planted in Spring 2014 and this is when The Lemon Tree & Co. started off as a fine dining restaurant and bar. We have now expanded into a diverse set of brands, catering to a simple and sophisticated lifestyle. From restaurants and bars to beach clubs, boutique hotels and residences; and today The Lemon Tree & Co. continues to create remarkable events and also has its iconic retail concept store.

At The Lemon Tree & Co. Group, we celebrate the artists, the dreamers, the hard workers and the achievers. Our main drive is our passion, and through passion we create the things we love, that consequently have a big role in the market. Your next career step could be a life- changing journey. We encourage our team to explore their talents and always keep learning because that's the beauty of it all.

Bring your passion, your dreams, your skills and ideas to the table. The Lemon Tree & Co. is a homey community and we consider each team member as part of the family. Take the chance to explore your career path.
